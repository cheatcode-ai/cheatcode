# Pitch Deck Reference

## Investor Narrative

A strong deck has a single story:

1. Pain is urgent and specific.
2. Product solves the pain in a differentiated way.
3. Market is large enough and accessible.
4. The team has a credible wedge.
5. The ask funds clear milestones.

## Market Sizing

Use public market data only when the definition matches the user's category. If not, use bottoms-up sizing:

```text
target accounts * realistic penetration * annual contract value = serviceable market
```

Label estimates and show assumptions in speaker notes or appendix.

## Visual QA Rules

- Title makes a claim.
- Body supports one claim only.
- Every slide has a visual or numeric callout.
- Text does not overflow.
- Citations are readable on research-heavy slides.
- Ask slide includes amount, runway, and milestone outcomes.
