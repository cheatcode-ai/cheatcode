# Social Post Pack Reference

## Platform Constraints

| Platform | Guidance |
|---|---|
| LinkedIn | Professional, specific, avoid empty inspiration language |
| X/Twitter | Short hooks, thread structure, avoid dense paragraphs |
| Reddit | Community-first tone, minimal promotion, concrete details |
| Instagram | Visual-first caption, carousel beats, simple CTA |

## Claim Safety

- Do not invent numbers, customer names, endorsements, awards, or availability.
- Mark speculative posts as "draft" when source review is needed.
- Preserve disclaimers from regulated or financial source material.
- Avoid "first", "only", or "best" unless sourced.

## Variant Pattern

For each platform provide:

1. Primary draft.
2. Alternate hook.
3. CTA.
4. Optional image or carousel prompt.
5. Risk note when a claim needs review.
