# Deep Research Reference

## Source Quality Ladder

1. Primary source: official docs, standards, filings, datasets, academic paper, transcript, direct publication.
2. Reputable secondary source: major publication, specialist analyst, established trade publication.
3. Community evidence: GitHub issues, forums, social posts, Reddit, personal blogs.

Use community evidence for implementation gotchas and sentiment, not as the only proof for factual claims.

## Citation Style

Use inline citations like `[[1]]` near the claim they support. End with:

```markdown
## Sources

1. Title - URL
2. Title - URL
```

Avoid bunching all citations at paragraph ends if individual sentences make different claims.

## Synthesis Checklist

- Scope and assumptions are explicit.
- The answer distinguishes facts, estimates, and inference.
- Contradictions are explained by date, source quality, geography, or definition.
- The conclusion includes confidence and what would change it.
